What is lieglex?

Lieglex is a software that helps organizations like NGOs, prisons, company cafeterias, hospitals, schools, and shelters manage food more efficiently.

Using math-based optimization, we create balanced meal plans at the lowest possible cost, reducing waste and supporting the Zero Hunger (UN SDG 2) goal, especially in developing countries.
